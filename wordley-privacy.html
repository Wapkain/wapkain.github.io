Wordley Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Short version: Wordley does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The game runs entirely on your device. There are no accounts, no ads, no analytics, no tracking.

1. Information We Collect

None. Wordley does not collect any personally identifiable information. We do not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any data to play the game.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following information locally on your device only. This data never leaves your phone:

  • Your game progress and statistics (wins, streaks, attempts)
  • Your language preference
  • App settings (theme, sound preferences)

Uninstalling the app removes all of this data permanently.

3. Permissions

Wordley does not request access to your contacts, camera, microphone, location, photos, or any other sensitive permissions.

4. Third-Party Services

Wordley does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Children's Privacy

Wordley is suitable for all ages. Since we do not collect any data, no special protections for children's data are required. The app is COPPA-compliant by design.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we ever add features that require data collection (for example, optional cloud sync), we will update this policy and notify users through an app update before the change takes effect.
